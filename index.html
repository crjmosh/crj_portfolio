SUPER RUDY BLOCK

utahjazz.com/games/srb

Super Rudy Block was a fun way to give fans of the Utah Jazz and Rudy Gobert an interactive entertainment experience on the Utah Jazz website.

I wrote the code that powers the gameplay and all menus using JavaScript and jQuery. It was written with all screen sizes in mind and provides a great experience on desktop and mobile devices.

This was an amazing project that involved many departments in the Utah Jazz organization and allowed me to tap into my lifelong passion of creating games.

UTAH JAZZ HOME PAGE

utahjazz.com

The Utah Jazz home page had many sections that were static and controlled by the NBA, taking away our ability to customize them how we wanted.

I rebuilt the home page from the ground up by creating dynamic, reusable components that we had full control over and could rearrange as we saw fit.

This became the first time the Utah Jazz home page had live-updated trending news content and the first time Jazz merchandise was displayed on the home page.

JAZZ SEASON TICKETS

utahjazz.com/tickets/seasontickets

The Utah Jazz season tickets page was the first full project I wrote using Vue.js after learning it in my free time. It involved many moving parts, including variable pricing and dynamic inventory availability.

It features a season ticket wizard that assists with finding the best ticket option for you. The seating map and various sections of the page update as you interact with the wizard to visually indicate your recommended seating section and show you the relevant benefits.

SEVENTEEN

Google Play Store

Seventeen is a number-puzzle game I created and the first game I published to the Google Play Store. I woke up one day with the concept for the game and decided to learn everything necessary to bring it to life.

All concepts, graphics, algorithms and text were created by me. I used Clickteam Fusion 2.5 to produce this game and export it for publish on the Google Play Store.

SUSHI ME! CARD GAME

I came up with the idea for Sushi Me! Card Game while looking at a sushi menu trying to decide what to get. All rules, gameplay mechanics, logos and card layouts were created by me. The sushi roll and ingredient illustrations were drawn by local artist Joe Bowen.

After some additional playtesting, the plan is to create a crowd-funding campaign and release the card game out into the world.
